== Description ==

Taggable allows you to tag your Facebook friends on Pages and Posts. You can tag Facebook friends from your Admin panel.  You can also control the way that tags appear on your blog.

You also have the option to send notifications of new tags into your Facebook news feed and/or your Twitter feed.

== Installation ==

Upload the Taggable plugin to your blog, activate it, and tag your friends in your posts.
